Morning Routine

Each morning I have a couple of very tiny atomic habits that I do in order to chip away at goals or areas of growth that I have in mind for myself. Currently there's only three, and I change them out for others as I reach my goals. So some of the ones I'm doing currenlty I have been doing for a while now, while others I've just recently cycled into my morning routine.

So allow me to share what they are and how I budget time for them each day.

To get started I use a Pomodoro Timer to make sure I am actually using the full time I've budgeted for each habit practice task. The specific timer I use is called, Flow. I like it because you can set your intervals and rounds to fit your needs.

The first area of improvement I have been working on for a few months now is my typing accuracy and speed. I've been notoriously slow and inaccurate in my past and finally decided to take a go at changing that. So for about 2 months and a few weeks now I practice for five minutes every morning using a site called, TypingClub.

My next area of focus has been getting back to practicing drums. It's been a while since I was serious about practicing drums and I can tell a difference lol. So each morning I set aside ten to fifteen minutes to practice various skills

So this concludes my little bit about my atomic habits.
